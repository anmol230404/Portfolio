# Figma AI Prompt — "Flight Beyond Horizons" Premium Portfolio

Design a premium, cinematic, emotionally engaging, highly interactive personal portfolio website inspired by the journey of a bird flying freely through an endless sky. The website should symbolize ambition, growth, freedom, curiosity, exploration, learning, and continuously reaching new heights.

Use my uploaded photo as the central visual identity of the website and build the entire experience around the metaphor of flight.

## Design Philosophy

This should NOT feel like a traditional developer portfolio.

It should feel like an interactive story where visitors accompany a bird on its journey from its first flight to soaring above the clouds.

Every section should represent a new altitude, a new chapter, and a new achievement in my journey.

The experience should feel:

* Inspirational
* Dreamy
* Elegant
* Premium
* Emotional
* Memorable
* Smooth
* Modern

Think:

* Apple-level simplicity
* Awwwards storytelling
* Luxury editorial design
* Nature-inspired animations
* Modern SaaS polish
